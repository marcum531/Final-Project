What Would've Been? What Would 2020 Employment Rates Have Looked Like If the Pandemic Hadn't Happened?

The purpose of this project was to examine unemployment trends in the United States over the past 5 years, and to compare those numbers to a predictive model of what employment in 2020 might've looked like if the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic hadn't occurred.

To accomplish this, we examined data from 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, and 2019 to better understand employment trends in the United States. We examined this on the state level and also more specifically on a granular level within three different counties of Ohio.
